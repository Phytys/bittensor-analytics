About This Project

Bittensor Subnet Analytics is an open-source project exploring the decentralized AI economy. We track subnet performance using live blockchain data and share our findings through an interactive dashboard and a research journal.

Project Status

Phase 1 Complete: We've successfully deployed our initial platform with:

  • Real-time subnet analytics dashboard
  • Basic scoring model with normalized metrics
  • Research journal documenting our methodology
  • Secure production deployment on Heroku

Phase 2 (In Progress): We're expanding our analysis to include:

  • Alpha APY and emission sustainability metrics
  • Subnet holder distribution analysis
  • Enhanced scoring models
  • More comprehensive market insights

For detailed insights into our methodology and progress, visit our research journal.
